Return Policy

Thank you for choosing Helmet Cases for your purchase.

We understand that sometimes items may not meet your expectations, and we are committed to making the return process as easy as possible.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of the original purchase date.
  • The product must be unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition as received.
  • Proof of purchase, such as a receipt or order confirmation, is required for all returns.

Return Procedure:

  1. Contact our customer service team at customer_service@example.com to initiate the return process.
  2. Clearly state the reason for the return and provide relevant details about the item.
  3. Once your return request is approved, you will receive instructions on how to return the item.

Refund Process:

  • Upon receiving the returned item, our team will inspect it to ensure it meets the eligibility criteria.
  • You will be notified of the status of your refund via email.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7 days of receiving the returned item.
  • The refund will be credited to the original payment method used during the purchase.

Exchange Policy:

If you wish to exchange an item, please follow the return procedure, and then place a new order for the desired item.

Damaged or Defective Items:

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately. We will arrange for a replacement or provide a full refund, including any applicable shipping costs.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about our return policy, our customer service team is here to assist you. Reach out to us at info@example.com or call Customer Service Phone.

Your satisfaction is our priority, and we appreciate your trust in Helmet Cases.
